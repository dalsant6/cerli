ClockIt Work Data Tracker

Track, analyze, and export your work hours, pay, and tax withholdings.

Build

ClockIt was built for Android native in Java, utilizing local database SQLite.

Calculate

For hourly employees, calculating your paycheck can be difficult due to complex tax withholdings. With built in Federal and state tax tables, calculating your next paycheck is intuitive and accurate. Additional withholding options allow the user to set city and county tax rates as well.

Analyze

Use more than 40 graphing methods to analyze your pay, hours, and tax withholdings. Tipped employees find this feature quite useful to figure out which days are most profitable.

Export

An all inclusive work tracker wouldn't be complete without the ability to export the data that's been aggregated. ClockIt allows it users to create reports in PDF format and send them via email or text messaging.
