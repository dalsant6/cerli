Shift

Jungle Themed Puzzle Game

Launch
Shift was launched Christmas morning of 2015 on the Google Play app market. Within the first week, more than 25,000 users were registered.
Synced Accounts
Local database coordinates with the online database to allow players to play offline and pick up where they left off on multiple devices using the same account.
Ads Optimized
Self built library included to check availability and reward rate for advertisements from more than 10 different providers. It then queues up advertisements from the top three providers and shows the top of the stack when needed. This results in a flawless 100% fill rate with the best CPM(cost per mille).
Build
Shift is built over the course of a year in Android native Java. The APK is minimized to less than 20MB due to it's efficient level generation and graphics implementation.
Level Generation
Algorithm utilizes AI to create and check viability of each level to determine it's difficulty. This made it possible to create incredibly complex levels in just seconds, store them in a format that takes up less than a kilobyte, and then parse them in app to generate the level.
Data Storage
Shift implements Parse database services for user authentication and online storage, as well as SQLite for local storage.
